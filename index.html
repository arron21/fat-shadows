Fat Shadows on Images

In order to apply a fat shadow on an image you can either apply it to a parent div, or the image itself.

<div class="fat-shadow-parent">
   <img src="https://picsum.photos/200/300" />
</div>
<img class="fat-shadow" src="https://picsum.photos/200/300" />
